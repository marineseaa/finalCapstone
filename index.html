Why photography is a great hobby

My name is Marina. Photography has been my hobby for quite a long time. It filled my life in a wonderful fulfilling way and helped me to discover and develop a creative side of me. Photography taught me to notice my world and to be aware of the wonderful little things that surround us each day.

Photography can help you to capture anyting which is important for you. It's not only a memory, but it can bring you back to the feelings of that particular moment.

You can take up photography as a hobby at any time, any age and with any camera. In this blog I am going to share a lot of useful inforamtion and tips about photography.
